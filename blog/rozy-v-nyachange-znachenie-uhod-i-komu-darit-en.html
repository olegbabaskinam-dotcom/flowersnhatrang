Roses in Nha Trang: Meaning, Care and Who to Give Them To

The rose is the most universal flower — given for birthdays, anniversaries, as a sign of love or for no reason at all. But behind a beautiful bouquet lies a whole language: the colour and number of roses say more than you might think. In this article we cover what roses mean, who they suit and how to keep a bouquet fresh in Nha Trang's hot climate.

What the colour of roses means

The colour of a rose is its main message. By choosing a shade, you speak about your feelings before you even add a card:

  • ❤️ Red — love and passion. The classic choice for a partner, a confession or a romantic surprise.
  • 🤍 White — purity, respect and admiration. Fitting for a wedding, an anniversary or as a sign of sincerity.
  • 🌸 Pink — tenderness and gratitude. A good choice for a mother, a friend or the start of a relationship.
  • 💜 Lilac and purple — admiration and something special. Perfect when you want to surprise with an unusual bouquet.

You can mix shades too: white-and-pink arrangements look soft and suit almost any occasion.

How many roses to give

In the tradition of gifting, the number matters — and so does whether it is odd or even. Bouquets are given with an odd number of flowers; an even number is reserved for mourning.

  • 💐 25 roses — a neat, versatile bouquet for a birthday or no special reason.
  • 🌹 51 roses — a generous gift that is sure to impress.
  • 👑 101 roses — a grand gesture for an anniversary, a confession or an important milestone.

If in doubt, let the occasion guide you: the bigger the moment, the bigger the bouquet. And a basket of 101 roses delivered straight to a hotel room creates an effect that is remembered for a long time.

Who to give roses to and for what occasion

Roses are wonderful because they suit almost everyone — only the colour and size of the bouquet change:

  • 💞 Wife or girlfriend — red or white-and-pink roses for an anniversary, a birthday or a confession.
  • 🌷 Mother — gentle pink or white roses as a sign of warmth and gratitude.
  • 🤝 Friend or colleague — light shades, not overloaded with romance.
  • ✨ Just because — a small bouquet for no reason often delights more than a gift for a date.

On holiday, roses are a great way to surprise someone: meet them with a bouquet at the airport, or send it to their room while they are at the beach.

How to keep roses fresh in Nha Trang's heat

The hot, humid climate is the main enemy of cut flowers. But with simple care, roses easily last several days, even on holiday:

  • ✂️ Trim the stems at an angle by 1–2 cm under running water before placing them in a vase.
  • 💧 Change the water every day and remove any leaves that sit below the water line.
  • ❄️ Keep the bouquet away from direct sun and closer to the air conditioner — coolness extends freshness.
  • 🌫 In strong heat, mist the buds with a spray bottle in the morning and evening.

We arrange each bouquet on the delivery day, so the roses arrive as fresh as possible — after that, it all comes down to care.
