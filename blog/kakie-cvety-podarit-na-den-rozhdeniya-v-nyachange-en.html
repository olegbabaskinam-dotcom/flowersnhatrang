What Flowers to Gift for a Birthday in Nha Trang

A birthday in Nha Trang is a perfect reason to surprise someone with fresh flowers. But which bouquet is both beautiful and fitting? In this guide we cover the most popular choices, explain what the numbers mean and show how to get same-day delivery straight to a hotel or villa.

The most popular birthday flowers

Roses are a safe bet — festive and suitable for almost any occasion. But a birthday also welcomes warmer, more personal choices.

  • 🌹 Classic roses — 25 stems in elegant wrapping: timeless and striking.
  • 🌷 Peony-style roses — lush and soft, perfect for a romantic greeting.
  • 🌸 Lilies — fragrant and refined for a more understated bouquet.
  • 🧺 A basket of 101 roses — a true wow gift with no vase needed.

Not sure what to pick? Message us and we will suggest what is freshest and most beautiful right now.

Match the bouquet to the person

A great gift reflects the person. A few simple cues:

  • For a romantic partner — peony roses in soft tones.
  • For someone bold and bright — deep red or purple roses.
  • For a mother or elder — a flower basket: elegant and vase-free.
  • For a friend or colleague — a tasteful mixed bouquet or lilies.

Colour matters too: pink for tenderness, red for love and admiration, white for respect, purple for originality.

How many flowers to give and what numbers mean

By tradition flowers are given in odd numbers, and the bigger the bouquet, the stronger the gesture:

  • 25 roses — a warm yet modest greeting.
  • 51 roses — an expressive gift for someone close.
  • 101 roses — maximum impact, admiration and deep respect.

Add a bunch of helium balloons and you get a ready-made party setup and a great photo spot.
