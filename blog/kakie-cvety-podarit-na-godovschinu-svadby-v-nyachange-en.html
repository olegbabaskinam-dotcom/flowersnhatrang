What Flowers to Gift for a Wedding Anniversary in Nha Trang

A wedding anniversary in Nha Trang is a special day, and flowers say more than words. But which bouquet truly conveys your love and suits the moment? In this guide we cover what flowers to give for an anniversary, what their colour and number mean, and how to get same-day delivery straight to a hotel or villa.

The best anniversary flowers

The main anniversary symbol is red roses — a sign of love and passion. But there are softer, more personal options to match a couple's mood.

  • 🌹 Red roses — the anniversary classic: love that never fades.
  • 🌷 Peony-style roses — lush and romantic for a tender date.
  • 🤍 White roses and lilies — loyalty, purity and respect.
  • 🧺 A basket of 101 roses — for a milestone and a true wow effect.

Not sure what to pick? Message us and we will suggest what is freshest and most beautiful right now.

Match the bouquet to your story

A great anniversary bouquet reflects the couple. A few simple cues:

  • For a romantic surprise for your wife — red or peony roses.
  • For a tender, warm date — pink and white roses in soft tones.
  • For a big milestone — a large basket: elegant and vase-free.
  • For an understated, elegant gesture — white lilies.

Colour matters too: red for love and passion, pink for tenderness, white for loyalty and purity, purple for admiration and the uniqueness of your bond.

How many flowers to give and what numbers mean

By tradition flowers are given in odd numbers, and the bigger the bouquet, the stronger the gesture:

  • 25 roses — a warm and romantic greeting.
  • 51 roses — an expressive gift for the one you love.
  • 101 roses — the ultimate declaration of love for a milestone date.

Add a bunch of helium balloons with hearts and you get a ready-made celebration setup and a beautiful photo spot for the couple.
